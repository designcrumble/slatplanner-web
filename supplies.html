Printer

We highly recommend keeping a dedicated color printer in the planning area for printing unscheduled (backlog) tasks at a moment’s notice.

We recommend the HP Envy 5660 for under a hundred dollars. It’s wireless which means you can print from Android, and supports AirPrint ® which means you can print from any iPhone or iPad.

Slat Wall or Slat Board

SlatNotes mechanically attach to slat walls, so your pieces will never lose their stick no matter how many times you move them. They create clean rows and make it easy to slide groups of activities together.

Slat boards come in multiple styles and finishes and can be seamlessly combined to create a collaborative wall space as large as you need. Extra slat-wall space can also be used to hang anything! They are available locally at retail fixture supply stores in any city.
